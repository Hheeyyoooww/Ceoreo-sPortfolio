About Me

Hey, I’m Nelly Ulya. A Visual Communication Design student at Universitas Negeri Semarang, 22 years old and proudly from Magelang. I’m obsessed with photography, videography, and occasionally getting lost in illustration. I see visuals as more than just pretty things. They’re stories, vibes, and quiet strategies that speak louder than words. Whether it’s behind the camera or on a blank canvas, I create with intention—and a bit of edge. I’ve always been drawn to how visuals can make people feel—nostalgia, curiosity, even joy. That’s why I do what I do. Scroll through, and let the visuals do the talking. Got a story to tell? Let’s make something awesome together!
